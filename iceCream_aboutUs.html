What is ice cream?

Ice cream is a sweetened frozen food typically eaten as a snack or dessert. It may be made from dairy milk or cream and is flavoured with a sweetener, either sugar or an alternative, and any spice, such as cocoa or vanilla. Frozen desserts originate as far back as 550 BCE, though ice cream as we know it was populariezed in the 1800s.
