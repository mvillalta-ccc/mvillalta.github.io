Matt's Ice Cream was established in 2030. We strive to serve delicious ice cream at an affordable price.

Matt's Ice Cream was born out of a daydream Matt had one day. He imagined a world where good ice cream wasn't $7 a pint. Since then, we've done our best to provide quality ice cream at budget prices. We use only organic, local ingredients and make our ice cream in small batches.

The Matt's Ice Cream team consists of our founder, Matt, and his partner Maddy, as well as our top notch scoopers and ice cream makers.
